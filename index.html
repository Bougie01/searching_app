Pipeline Design

From cleaned records to category suggestions.

Structured schema

Extract titles, descriptions, categories, materials, and product traits into a consistent feature layer that can be reused across review, clustering, and supervised models.

Embeddings + attributes

Blend text embeddings with structured fields such as category, fit, and key specs to outperform text-only grouping on noisy catalog data.

Outliers and quality

Detect suspicious or emerging product types by comparing predicted categories, nearest neighbors, and generic or incomplete metadata.
